Seed Packets: Tear, Toss, Trust

Our pocket-sized seed packets make reforestation accessible to everyone. Each packet contains region-specific, fast-growing seeds that transform empty spaces into thriving ecosystems.

📦

Tear

Open the compact, biodegradable packet with ease. Designed to fit in your pocket, wallet, or bag for whenever inspiration strikes.

🌱

Toss

Gently scatter the seeds in any empty patch of earth you encounter during your daily walks or travels. No special skills required.

🌳

Trust Nature

Let nature work its magic. With rain and sunlight, your small act grows into trees, shade, oxygen, and life for our planet.

🌱

Our Beginning

Our journey started with a simple question: "What if doing good for the planet was as effortless as breathing?" From that thought, our unique concept was born.

Pocket-Sized Revolution

Pocket-sized seed packets that anyone can carry and throw into empty spaces while walking, traveling, or exploring. Each packet is just 2x2 inches in size, light to carry, and filled with fast-growing, resilient seeds chosen specially for different regions.

🌿

Simple Process

The process is beautifully simple: Tear → Toss → Trust Nature
